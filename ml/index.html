Sometimes in political science, we are more concerned with prediction accuracy, rather than causality or explainability. These are notes for a rigorous introduction to machine learning prediction methods that have been adopted in the social sciences.

Prerequisites: Topics covered in Intro to Statistical Inference and Regression Analysis. Some working knowledge of R and Maths to the level of Workshop: R Fundamentals and Maths for Political Science is also helpful.
